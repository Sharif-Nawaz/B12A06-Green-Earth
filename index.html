About the Campaign

Community‑led tree planting

Green Earth mobilizes students, families, and local partners to plant climate‑smart trees in urban and rural areas. Every contribution helps restore biodiversity, cool cities, and create habitat.

  • Native species selected for each region
  • Transparent reporting of trees planted
  • Education for long‑term tree care
